communion fancy

description

I interpreted communion in a religious way and created a goblet that can either be used for wine or bread. Old goblets are usually golden with valuable stones and decorative lines so I concentrated on giving the traditional piece a more simple, modern but also fancy look.


process

After ideating and creating a simple 3D model I went to the metal workshop to build the goblet. It is made from copper which was heated to make it softer for hammering it into shape. After molding it, the bowl was again heated and rinsed with cold water to create a random pattern. The stem was polished and then soldered to the bowl.


experience

At first I had difficulties with the words. What does communion mean? What defines fancy? But after I freed my mind a bit and thought more openly it was fun to create something I would not usually create. In my studies we never really designed for religious purposes, objects that had a hidden value. I liked reimagining the traditional piece and creating something more modern in contrast to old religions.
