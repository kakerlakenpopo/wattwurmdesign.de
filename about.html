why the name?

the wattwurm (engl: sandworm) does one thing really well: shitting. Due to this action it helps the eco system by bringing nutritions to the surface and pumping air into the ground. Like the worm wattwurm design will produce projects like shit, digging up new ideas and letting fresh wind blow trough the design world and its strategies.

what is the purpose?

​wattwurm design is a self initiated project in wich I want to experiment with the time for  a design and wether it is visible that it was created in one day, one month or half a year. The idea was created on november 27th in 2018 when I wondered why I always got scared of projects I had to do in my studies, especially finishing or sticking to them. This experiment will hopefully help me to create some techniques to structure my work or to realize how I work most efficient and fun.

how does it work?

The goal is to create a design in 24 hours from two random things - a need and an adjective.
