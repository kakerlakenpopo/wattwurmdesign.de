companionship exaggerated

description

a companion is there for you in times in need. A companion gives you advice and comforts you when you have troubles. This website gives people the chance to contact a virtual companion whenever needed - 24/7 if wanted. But in the end it is still just a machine. It will not remember anything you said but it can help you to give yourself feedback by reflecting on your situation.
Try it yourself: click here.


process

I tried to figure out what the essence of the words are. I translated them to german (hence I am german) to get background information and put them into context. Talking to friends really helped me to ideate. We mostly brainstormed verbally about things that already existed and what kind of project would be possible to implement with these words.


experience

Talking to people really helps. More minds, more ideas. Since I was busy during these 24 hours I could not spend a long time ideating and grabbed one of the first ideas to implement it. I generated a small website and put it online wich is fun doing in a short time. Honestly I have to say that I am not fully convinced with this project. Since the concept of wattwurmdesign is quantity over quality one has to start with something - even though it might not be as cool as you originally hoped.
